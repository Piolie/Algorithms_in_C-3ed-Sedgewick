/* ---------------------------------------------------------------------
                              EXERCISE 2.26
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Suppose that H_k = N. Give an approximate formula that expresses k as a
function of N.
--------------------------------------------------------------------- */

Since H_N ~= ln N + O(1):
    H_k ~= ln k + O(1) = N
thus
    ln k ~= N - O(1)
         ~= N
and
    k ~= e^N
